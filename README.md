### 🚀 Architecting the Future of Real-World Assets (RWA)
I am building the digital bridge between India's physical real estate and the blockchain economy. My mission is to democratize high-value assets through **fractional ownership**, **T+0 settlement layers**, and government-verified on-chain land records.

---

### 🔍 Current Focus
- **🏢 RWA Infrastructure:** Engineering the core platform for **TokenizingIndia**, enabling liquidity for premium Indian real estate.
- **🛡️ AI & Cybersecurity:** Leveraging **Claude Mythos** and Agentic LLMs to automate smart contract vulnerability research and autonomous patching.
- **📜 Land-Tech:** Mapping **ULPIN** (Unique Land Parcel Identification Number) and **Bhoomi** records to decentralized ledgers for tamper-proof title verification.
